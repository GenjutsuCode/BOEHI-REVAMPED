The Visionary Behind the Mission

OUR STORY

The BOEHI Foundation exists to honor and extend the work of Dr. Bode Olajumoke, a visionary statesman, educator, and philanthropist whose life’s work centers on lifting others through knowledge and service. His impact is seen not only in the policies he shapes but in the thousands of lives being transformed today by his commitment to human capital development.

What began as a tribute to Dr. Olajumoke’s enduring influence has evolved into a growing movement dedicated to empowering Nigeria’s most vulnerable populations. Guided by his active vision, BOEHI continues to bridge the gap—one scholarship, one skill, and one life at a time.

"Govt. alone can’t fund education adequately"

— Sen. Bode Olajumoke

OUR MISSION

To transform lives and communities by providing equitable access to quality education and practical hospitality skills for underserved children, youth, and women in Nigeria—fostering self-reliance, inclusion, and generational impact.

OUR VISION

A nation where every individual—regardless of background—has the tools, training, and support to learn, lead, and live with dignity.
We envision empowered communities built on knowledge, economic freedom, and the human capacity to rise.
